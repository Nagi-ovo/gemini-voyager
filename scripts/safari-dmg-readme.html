Voyager — Safari Upgrade

The app was renamed from “Gemini Voyager” to “Voyager”. Please migrate once.

Before you drag the app across: quit Safari, move the old “Gemini Voyager.app” to the Trash, then drag “Voyager.app” here. Don’t keep both apps. Your data (folders, prompts, settings) is preserved.

English

  1. Quit Safari completely (⌘Q).
  2. Drag the old “Gemini Voyager.app” from Applications to the Trash.
  3. Drag “Voyager.app” into Applications, then enable “Voyager Extension” in Safari → Settings → Extensions.

Don’t keep both apps, and don’t use Safari’s “Uninstall”.
